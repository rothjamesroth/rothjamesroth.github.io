Our Mission

Roth Technical Partners exists to empower American innovators and entrepreneurs by providing practical, reliable, and strategically aligned mechanical engineering. We transform ideas into safe, functional, and commercially viable solutions by combining technical expertise with real-world business strategy. Across the United States, there is tremendous untapped potential. Too often, promising ideas stall due to technical barriers, unclear development pathways, regulatory complexity, or misalignment between engineering execution and business goals. Roth Technical Partners removes those obstacles. We partner with our clients to unlock potential by delivering respectful, transparent, and results-driven engineering services tailored to their vision and objectives. By integrating ambitious, professional engineering with MBA-level strategic thinking, we help clients build reliable products, systems, and solutions that move ideas forward with confidence. We are committed to helping clients pursue new approaches, overcome technical challenges, and achieve meaningful results.
